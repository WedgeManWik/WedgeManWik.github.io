Magic Gate

Unreal Engine 5, C++, Blueprints, Gameplay Ability Systems


Game Description

This idea is a Strategic Turn-based Rogue-like dungeon-crawler magic card deck-building RPG game. When the player loads up the game, they pick a character, build a deck which they will play with, and enter the dungeon. To complete the dungeon, the player must go through the rooms of the dungeon, which can contain monster encounters, and reach the boss at the end of the dungeon. If they defeat the boss, they win. If they die at any point in the dungeon, they lose all their progress from the dungeon run. The main inspirations behind this idea are games such as Slay the Spire, Dungeon Mayhem and Hearthstone.
