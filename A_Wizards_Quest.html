A Wizard's Quest

HTML5, JavaScript, p5, repl.it


This is a project which I made over the summer break before starting university. I wanted to make a rogue-like RPG where the player explores a forest filled with various enemies with the aim of finding the treasure of the giant goblin.

The player can kill enemies with the projectiles they summon, and loot the gold the enemies drop - this gold can be spent on upgrades in the shop. Once the player has reached the last level, they have to face off and kill the giant goblin who protects its treasure. The game ends when the player dies or defeats the last boss.
